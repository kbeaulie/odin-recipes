Kody's Old Fashioned Cocktail

Description

The Old Fashioned is a classic cocktail that is easy to make but hard to master

Ingredients

  • 2 Ounces of Bourbon or Rye, Preferably High Proof
  • 1/2 Ounce of Real Maple Syrup
  • 2-3 Dashes of Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Large Cube of Ice or Ice Sphere
  • 1 Luxardo Cherry
  • 1 Orange

Directions

  1. Place ice cube into glassware of your choice, preferably a rocks glass
  2. Add 1/2 ounce of maple syrup to glass
  3. Add 2 ounces of Bourbon or Rye to glass
  4. Add 2-3 dashes of Angostura Bitters to glass
  5. Stir 20 times
  6. Using a knife, cut off a strip of orange peel and squeeze or express the orange peel into the drink
  7. Garnish with Luxardo Cherry and orangle peel
