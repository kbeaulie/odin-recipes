Whiskey Sour

Description

The Whiskey Sour is another classic cocktail that is incredibly delicous when made properly

Ingredients

  • 1 Ounce of Bourbon (I prefer Bourbon over Rye in this cocktail due to the extra sweetness Bourbon brings!)
  • 1 Ounce of Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • 1 Ounce of Egg Whites
  • 1/2 Ounce of Real Maple Syrup
  • Bitters
  • Luxardo Cherries
  • Boston Shaker with Strainer
  • Rocks Glass
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Add Bourbon, Lemon Juice, Egg Whites, and Maple Syrup to Boston Shaker and dry shake for 10 seconds
  2. Add ice to shaker and shake until metal becomes frosty
  3. Strain into chilled rocks glass
  4. Garnish with 1-2 dashes of bitters and speared luxardo cherry
