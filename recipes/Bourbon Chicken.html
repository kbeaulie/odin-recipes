Bourbon Chicken

Description

This delicous crispy chicken Dish is named not only after the type of whiskey used in it, but also the street that the recipe was first made on!

Ingredients

  • 4 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts, Halved
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Ginger
  • 4 Ounces Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Dried Minced Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 3/8 Cup Bourbon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Place chicken breasts on a baking dish.
  2. In a small bowl combine the ginger, soy sauce, onion flakes, bourbon, and garlic powder. Mix together and pour the mixture over chicken.
  3. Cover dish and place in refrigerator, allow to marinate overnight.
  4. Preheat oven to 325 F
  5. Remove cover from dish and bake for 1 1/2 hours, basting frequently
